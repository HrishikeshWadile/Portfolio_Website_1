Deep Learning Projects

Lung Cancer Prediction
Nov 2024 - Dec 2024

  • Project Overview: Focused on predicting lung cancer categories—adenocarcinoma, squamous carcinoma, and benign tissues—using deep learning.
  • Model Architecture: Leveraged the EfficientNetB4 architecture in TensorFlow.
  • Data Augmentation: Applied augmentation techniques to mitigate overfitting.
  • Fine-Tuning Strategy: Unfroze the last 10 layers of the model, enabling fine-tuning for improved performance.
  • Results: Achieved 93% accuracy, with strong precision, recall, and F1-scores validating its capability to effectively distinguish between cancerous and benign tissues.
